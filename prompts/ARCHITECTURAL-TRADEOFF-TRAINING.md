# Architectural Tradeoff Training Prompt

Explain the tradeoffs between:

[APPROACH A]
vs
[APPROACH B]

For each approach analyze:

- Performance characteristics
- Scalability profile
- Failure modes
- Operational complexity
- Cost implications
- Organizational impact

Then answer:

- When would a Staff engineer prefer each approach?
- What signals indicate switching is necessary?

Prioritize depth over surface comparisons.
